About PADI

The Professional Association of Diving Instructors (PADI) is the world's leading scuba diver training organization.

With more than 6,600 dive centers and resorts, and 137,000 individual PADI Professionals worldwide, PADI training materials and services let you experience scuba diving from nearly anywhere.

Our Mission

The mission of the PADI AWARE Foundation is to conserve the underwater world through partnerships, actions, and education.
