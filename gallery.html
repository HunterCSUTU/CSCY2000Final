Rehabilitated Dolphins

Back in the ocean once more!

Luna and Neptune

During the rehabilitation process, two injured dolphins, Luna and Neptune, received personalized care and treatment from a multidisciplinary team of experts. Through medical interventions, physical therapy, and behavioral training, Luna and Neptune regained their strength and health. With dedicated support, they made remarkable progress, eventually returning to their natural habitat, where they now thrive once more.
